Colours

Primary colours

A set of neutralised branding colours for making content play a primary role whenever it is possible. Other colours should be used in moderation, to compliment the primary set.

Special colours

Slightly desaturated, to make sure they are easy on the eye, these colours are reserved only for special cases:

  • Never try to combine special colours in your designs;
